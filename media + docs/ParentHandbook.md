# Parent Handbook

## Welcome
Thank you for enrolling your child in our tennis program. This handbook outlines expectations and policies.

## Program Overview
- Classes: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced.
- Sessions: Group and private options.
- Duration: 1 hour each.

## Expectations
- **Attendance**: Notify in advance for absences.
- **Behavior**: Respectful conduct on court.
- **Equipment**: Bring water; optional personal racket.

## Policies
- **Payments**: R200 per session, due weekly.
- **Refunds**: No refunds after 48 hours.
- **Safety**: Review `SafetyGuidelines.md`.
- **Progress**: Monthly reports via email.

## Communication
- Use provided templates for updates.
- Contact coach for concerns.

## Tips for Parents
- Encourage practice at home.
- Discuss goals with your child.
- Attend sessions if possible.
