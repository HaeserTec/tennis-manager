# Communication Templates

## Overview
Use these templates for consistent, professional communication with parents and players. Customize with specific details.

## 1. Weekly Session Reminder
Subject: Tennis Session Reminder - [Date]

Dear [Parent Name],

This is a reminder that [Player Name]'s tennis session is scheduled for [Date, Time] at [Location].

Please arrive 15 minutes early. Bring water, appropriate clothing, and any personal equipment.

If unable to attend, please notify me by [Time] to allow for adjustments.

Best,  
[Your Name]  
Head Coach

## 2. Progress Report
Subject: Monthly Progress Report for [Player Name]

Dear [Parent Name],

Here's [Player Name]'s progress update for [Month]:

- Strengths: [List]
- Areas for Improvement: [List]
- Goals Achieved: [List]
- Next Goals: [List]

[Player Name] is in [Class] and [performing well/needs extra focus].

Please let me know if you'd like to discuss.

Best,  
[Your Name]

## 3. Payment Notice
Subject: Payment Due for [Player Name]

Dear [Parent Name],

The payment of $[Amount] for [Period] is due by [Date].

Payment methods: [List options, e.g., Cash, Venmo].

Thank you for your support!

Best,  
[Your Name]

## 4. Emergency Contact Update
Subject: Update Emergency Contact Info

Dear Parents,

Please reply with updated emergency contact information for your child, including name, relationship, and phone number.

This ensures safety during sessions.

Thank you.

Best,  
[Your Name]

## 5. Class Change Notification
Subject: Class Advancement for [Player Name]

Dear [Parent Name],

Based on recent assessments, [Player Name] is ready to advance to [New Class].

New schedule: [Details].

Congratulations!

Best,  
[Your Name]

## Tips
- Send via email or group messaging app.
- Keep tone friendly and informative.
- Maintain a master contact list in `PlayerProgress.md`.
