# Intermediate Lesson Plans

These are three general 1-hour lesson plans for intermediate players, adapted for private (1 player), semi-private (2 players), and group (3-5 players) sessions. Each plan includes a structured timeline with warm-up, main drills, and cool-down.

## Lesson Plan 1: Stroke Refinement and Spin

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Dynamic stretches, stroke shadow swings with spin focus.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Forehand/backhand topspin drills; slice practice; rally consistency.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, technique review, personal goals.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Partner stretches, shared spin demonstrations.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Crosscourt rallies with spin; feedback on form.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, discuss spin effectiveness.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group stretches, spin shadow swings.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Spin station rotations; group rallies.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, share spin tips.

## Lesson Plan 2: Volleys and Net Play

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Net approach drills without balls.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Volley technique; approach shot to volley transitions.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, volley accuracy notes.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Partner net warm-ups.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Volley exchanges; point play at net.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, feedback on volleys.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group net warm-ups.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Volley stations; mini-games.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, group discussion.

## Lesson Plan 3: Tactical Play and Pressure

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Tactical shadow swings.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Directional hitting; pressure rallies.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, strategy review.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Partner tactical discussions.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Point simulations; strategic rallies.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, analyze plays.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group tactical talks.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Team tactics drills; pressure games.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, debrief.
