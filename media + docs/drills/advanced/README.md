# Advanced Tennis Drills

## Overview
These drills are for advanced players aiming to maximize performance, precision, and competitive edge. Emphasis is on high-level technique, tactical awareness, and physical conditioning under match-like intensity.

## Equipment Needed
- High-quality tennis racket
- Premium tennis balls
- Full access to tennis court
- Stopwatch or timer
- Video recording device (phone/camera)
- Stats tracking app or notebook
- Water bottle and towel

## Warm-up (15-20 minutes)
- Intensive dynamic warm-up with sprints
- Stroke-specific warm-up with increasing speed
- Short rally exchanges

## Core Drills

### 1. High-Intensity Rally Control
- Sustained rallies with varied spins (topspin, slice, flat)
- Focus on court positioning and shot selection
- Target repetitions: 50+ shots per rally set
- Incorporate pressure situations

### 2. Footwork Refinement
- Advanced split-step and recovery drills
- Lateral movement patterns
- Transition drills between forehand and backhand
- Agility ladder work integrated with shots

### 3. Backhand Slice Transitions
- Practice slice backhands in defensive situations
- Transition to offensive shots from slice
- Work on depth and angle control
- Combine with approach and volley

### 4. Recovery and Conditioning
- Drills emphasizing quick recovery after shots
- Endurance rallies with minimal rest
- Interval training for stamina

## Key Principles
- Focus on high-intensity rally control with varied spins and court positioning.
- Drill emphasis: footwork refinement, backhand slice transitions, and recovery time.
- Expect win-loss goal tracking, detailed stats logging, and video analysis reviews.
- Include warm-up, core drill, and cool-down segments with target repetitions.

## Tips for Advanced Players
- Analyze every shot for optimization
- Use video review to identify weaknesses
- Maintain peak physical condition
- Practice mental toughness under fatigue

## Progress Tracking
- Detailed stats: first serve percentage, unforced errors, winners
- Win-loss goals for each drill session
- Video analysis with self and coach review
- Track improvements in speed, accuracy, and endurance

## Cool-down
- Comprehensive stretching routine
- Foam rolling for recovery
- Nutritional replenishment
- Session debrief and goal setting for next practice
