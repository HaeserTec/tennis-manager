# Advanced Lesson Plans

These are three general 1-hour lesson plans for advanced players, adapted for private (1 player), semi-private (2 players), and group (3-5 players) sessions. Each plan includes a structured timeline with warm-up, main drills, and cool-down.

## Lesson Plan 1: High-Intensity Rallies and Positioning

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Intensive dynamic stretches, fast shadow swings.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Sustained rallies with spin variation; court positioning focus.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, performance analysis, goals.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Partner high-energy stretches.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Competitive rallies; positioning challenges.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, discuss strategies.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group intensive warm-ups.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Multi-player rallies; positioning drills.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, group review.

## Lesson Plan 2: Footwork and Recovery Drills

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Agility ladder work.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Advanced footwork patterns; recovery after shots.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, footwork assessment.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Partner agility drills.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Mirror footwork; recovery rallies.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, feedback.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group agility circuits.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Footwork challenges; recovery games.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, share techniques.

## Lesson Plan 3: Competitive Strategy and Endurance

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Mental prep, fast swings.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Endurance rallies; strategic shot selection.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, strategy notes.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Partner strategy talks.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Competitive points; endurance tests.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, analyze matchups.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group strategy sessions.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Tournament-style games; endurance drills.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, debrief strategies.
