# Beginner Lesson Plans

These are three general 1-hour lesson plans for beginner players, adapted for private (1 player), semi-private (2 players), and group (3-5 players) sessions. Each plan includes a structured timeline with warm-up, main drills, and cool-down.

## Lesson Plan 1: Basic Strokes Introduction

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Light jogging, arm circles, shadow swings.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Forehand and backhand groundstrokes with slow feeds; focus on form and consistency.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretching, review key points, positive feedback.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group jogging, partner stretches, shared shadow swings.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Alternating forehand/backhand rallies; coach feeds balls; emphasize turn-taking.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Group stretching, discuss improvements, encourage each other.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Circle jogging, group arm circles, collective shadow swings.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Station-based drills (forehand station, backhand station); rotate every 10 min; coach supervision.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Group stretching, share one thing learned, high-fives.

## Lesson Plan 2: Footwork and Movement

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Dynamic stretches, easy footwork patterns.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Side-to-side movement drills with ball feeds; focus on split-step and balance.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Static stretches, review movement tips, goal setting.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Partner-led stretches, synchronized footwork.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Mirror drills (copy partner's movements); rally with movement emphasis.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Partner stretches, feedback exchange.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group dynamic stretches, line footwork.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Tag game with tennis balls; movement challenges in pairs/small groups.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Group stretches, discuss favorite drill.

## Lesson Plan 3: Serve and Fun Games

### Private Session (1 Player)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Serve toss practice without racket.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Serve technique breakdown; mini-games like target serving.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, serve accuracy recap.

### Semi-Private Session (2 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Partner toss drills.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Serve practice with feedback; simple point play.
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Stretches, compare serves.

### Group Session (3-5 Players)
- **Warm-up (10 min)**: Group toss games.
- **Main Drills (40 min)**: Serve stations; fun games like "serve and catch."
- **Cool-down (10 min)**: Group stretches, fun awards.
