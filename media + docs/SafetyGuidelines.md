# Safety Guidelines

## Overview
Prioritize safety in all tennis sessions to prevent injuries and ensure a positive experience for all players.

## General Rules
- Always warm up and cool down properly.
- Use appropriate court surfaces and equipment.
- Monitor weather conditions; cancel if unsafe.
- Have a first aid kit on site.

## Injury Prevention
- Teach proper techniques to avoid strain.
- Encourage hydration and breaks.
- Watch for signs of fatigue or pain.
- Adapt drills for players with limitations.

## Emergency Procedures
- Know location of nearest medical facility.
- Have emergency contacts for each player.
- In case of injury: Stop activity, assess, contact parent/medical help.
- Document incidents in player progress notes.

## Equipment Safety
- Inspect rackets and balls before use.
- Ensure nets are secure.
- Use cones/markers safely.

## Coach Responsibilities
- Maintain certifications (e.g., CPR if applicable).
- Supervise all activities closely.
- Communicate safety policies to parents.

## Review
- Update guidelines annually.
- Train assistants on safety protocols.
